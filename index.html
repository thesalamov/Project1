About Me

My name is Arzuman Salamov. I have been teaching Informatics at Shamakhi European High School for 1 year now. When I started working as a teacher at the high school, I wanted to leave a mark in my teaching career.I will organize a knowledge competition in the field of Informatics in Shamakhi district. I shared my thoughts about this with the director of our high school and he welcomed this idea.

The competition was scheduled for 26.05.2023. My team from our high school also participated in the competition. Our team name was "John Fon Neyman" . The race was very competitive and transparent.
